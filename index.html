Newspaper page recreation

Given a random page from the Metro newspaper, we were tasked with recreating it by producing a html document of our own and styling it to imitate the original as closely as possible. We worked as a group of 2 by dividing the page into sections and assigning them fairly to help split the workload evenly, before compiling them and styling the whole sheet to create the newspaper page in html format.
